Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that enables machines to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence. AI is used in smartphones, healthcare, education, banking, transportation, and many other industries.

Applications of AI

AI is becoming a part of our daily lives. Some common applications are:

  • Virtual Assistants (Siri, Google Assistant, Alexa)
  • Self-driving Cars
  • Medical Diagnosis
  • Online Shopping Recommendations
  • Face Recognition Systems

Advantages of AI

Artificial Intelligence helps improve productivity, reduces human errors, works continuously without getting tired, and provides faster decision-making.

Future of AI

In the future, AI will continue to transform industries by improving automation, enhancing healthcare, supporting education, and creating smarter technologies that make everyday life easier.
