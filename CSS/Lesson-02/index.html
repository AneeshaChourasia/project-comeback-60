Indore Public School

Founded in 1987, Indore Public School (IPS) is a premier, co-educational, English-medium CBSE school. Located on a sprawling 50-acre campus in Rajendra Nagar, about 9 km from the city center, it offers both day boarding and residential (hostel) facilities from Nursery to Class 12

Campus & Facilities

The school is designed to provide holistic, global-standard education. Key infrastructure includes:

  • Academic Hubs:

    Smart classrooms, well-equipped science and math labs, a language lab, and an Atal Tinkering Lab for robotics.

  • Library:

    A vast Learning Resource Center with digital access and e-books.

  • Boarding:

    Separate AC accommodations for boys and girls, complete with 24x7 medical care and nutritious multi-cuisine dining for up to 1,000 students.

  • Sports & Arts:

    Soundproof music and dance studios, plus facilities for swimming, boxing, and other indoor/outdoor sports.

  • Transportation:

    A fleet of 40 buses equipped with GPS tracking

Academics & Admissions

Affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), IPS follows the 5+3+3+4 schooling model. The curriculum emphasizes both academic rigor and co-curricular activities, shaping students with modern skills while retaining Indian values.

Prospective parents can check the specific age criteria on the IPS Admission Process page and can explore the distinct campuses via the IPS Main Website. To learn more about their unique features, you can also view the IPS Why Us or IPS Rajendra Nagar Profile for more details.
